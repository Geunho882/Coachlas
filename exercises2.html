Squat (High bar)

Quads

Glutes & Hamstrings

To do a high bar squat, place the bar on your upper traps, grip it slightly wider than shoulder-width, and stand with feet shoulder-width apart. Brace your core, keep your chest up, and lower yourself by bending your hips and knees, keeping your torso upright. Squat until your thighs are at least parallel, then push through your feet to stand back up, keeping your spine neutral and knees aligned.

Body setup

  • Feet shoulder-width, toes slightly out (5-15°)
  • Chest up, spine neutral
  • Eyes forward or slightly up
  • Weight centered on midfoot/heels

Movement

  • Brace core with a deep breath (Brace?)
  • Hips back, then lower down
  • Knees track over toes (no collapse)
  • Aim for thighs at or below parallel
  • Drive through heels to stand tall
